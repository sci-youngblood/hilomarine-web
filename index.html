Pacific Crossings Welcome

Hilo Harbor — Your First Stop in Hawaiʻi

Hilo Harbor is a major customs clearance point for international Pacific crossings. We provide fast, reliable marine services for vessels arriving from Tahiti, the Marquesas, mainland USA, and beyond. Call ahead or reach us on arrival.

Tahiti & French Polynesia Direct passage landfall. Typical 18–22 day Pacific crossing with Hilo as first US port of entry.
US West Coast ~2,100nm from California. Common shakedown passage for offshore cruisers heading to the South Pacific.
Customs & Border Protection Hilo is an official CBP port of entry. We can help coordinate service around your clearance window.
Radio Bay Anchorage Stern-to moorage and anchoring for arriving offshore vessels. We come to you.
Post-Passage Hull Cleaning Schedule a bottom scrub on arrival — remove tropical growth before moving on to the South Pacific or US mainland.
Running Gear Inspection Post-crossing prop, shaft, and zinc check with video documentation. Know what you're working with before you leave.
